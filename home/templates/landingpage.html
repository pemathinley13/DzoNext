Projects

Our web applications leverage advanced NLP techniques and machine learning to enable better communication, accessibility, and understanding of the Dzongkha language.

Dzongkha Translation

Facilitates the translation of text between Dzongkha and English with a focus on Dzongkha-to-English translations, breaking down language barriers.

Dzongkha ASR

Automatic Speech Recognition converts spoken Dzongkha into written text using advanced ASR technology.

Dzongkha Text-To-Speech

Converts written text into natural-sounding Dzongkha speech for an immersive audio experience.

About DzoNext

DzoNext is a collaborative effort aiming to preserve and promote Dzongkha, the national language of Bhutan, through cutting-edge NLP and AI technologies.
